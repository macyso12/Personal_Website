Rube-Goldberg Group 4 Project (Youtube)

This project requires my group member and I to built a contraption which is deliberately over-engineered to perform a simple task and linking with other groups in a complicated fashion to create a chain reaction.

For our group, we uses the principle of lever to activate the next ball to keep rolling forward, with a semlessly complex contraption, which will hit the first domino to create the domino effect, moving to a small chemical reaction at the side (elephant paste), which at the end trigger our connection with the other team to continue the sequence.
